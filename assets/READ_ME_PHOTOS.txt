To add MORE photos to Our Gallery without coding:
1. Put your new photo inside the assets folder.
2. Rename it exactly like one of these:
   gallery1.jpg
   gallery2.jpg
   gallery3.jpg
   ... up to gallery30.jpg

PNG also works:
   gallery1.png
   gallery2.png
   ... up to gallery30.png

To remove a photo:
- Delete that image from assets, or rename it to something else.

Important:
- Keep index.html and assets folder together.
- Names must match exactly.
- Use lowercase names like gallery1.jpg, not Gallery1.JPG.
